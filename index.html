I love webcomics made by passionate artists, bonus points if they have fluffy animals! So here's some of my favorite furry webcomics.

Dog Haus

Growing up is hard, having no motivation to do anything is even harder, and then having depression on top of it? Insurmountable.
But for Maggie? She's getting through it, one meeting with her therapist and wacky adventure with friends at a time.

You should check this comic out if...

  • Slice of Life is a genre you love.
  • You're struggiling coping with growing up.
  • Sarcasm and relateable moments are something you want to see more of!

Dogs on Dates

Gay people are cool, (I would know I am one), but what if they were dogs? Well look no further than Dogs on Dates!
Brad and Bernie navigate love and young adult life with whimsy and innocence, making the average reader go "huh... gay people are cool."

You should check this comic out if...

  • Slice of Life is a genre you love.
  • Romance is a fan favorite for you!
  • Sweet and Sappy moments fuel your being.

Duncan and Eddie

Gay people are cool part 2: Electric Boogaloo. Like Dogs on Dates, this webcomic follows a lovestruck goat and crocodile go through the motions of young love and chill adventures.
Right now in the comic there's even a side story call "Puppy Love"! This story too will have you saying "huh... gay people are cool."

You should check this comic out if...

  • Slice of Life is a genre you love.
  • Romance is a must in your fiction.
  • Fluff is in your top AO3 tags.
